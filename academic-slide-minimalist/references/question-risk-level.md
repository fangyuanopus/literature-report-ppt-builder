# Question Risk Level

Use this reference to judge how likely a slide claim is to be challenged and how cautious the wording should be.

## Risk Levels

### Low Risk

Use when the claim is directly supported by a main-paper figure, SI control, or direct measurement.

Wording can use:

```text
表明 | 显示 | 直接观察到 (only for direct observation)
```

### Medium Risk

Use when multiple pieces of evidence support the claim, but the conclusion includes interpretation or inference.

Wording should use:

```text
支持 | 说明 | 与...一致 | 共同支持
```

Prepare a short safe answer and cite the supporting figures in notes.

### High Risk

Use when the claim is proposed, mechanistic, indirect, limited to specific conditions, or relies heavily on modeling.

Wording should use:

```text
提示 | 可能 | 作者认为 | 该结果支持一种解释
```

High-risk claims need backup support or explicit limitation notes.

## Risk Table Template

```text
slide | claim | risk level | reason | wording rule | backup support | likely question
```

## Common High-Risk Claims

- mechanism is proven;
- industrial application is ready;
- this is universally generalizable;
- one figure alone identifies active site;
- performance comparison is fully fair across literature;
- simulation result is treated as experimental proof.
